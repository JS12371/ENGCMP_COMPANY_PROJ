Brand Story

TrueLink was founded in 2025 by a team of undergrads who became disillusioned with how platforms were designed to exploit human psychology rather than serve genuine needs.

We pledge to creating "true links" between people; connections that are meaningful, intentional, and enriching rather than superficial or manipulative.

Mission Statement

"To create digital spaces that enhance rather than replace human connection, fostering relationships that enrich lives both online and offline."

Our mission guides every decision we make, from product design to business practices. We measure our success not by engagement metrics or ad revenue, but by the quality of connections our platform facilitates.

About Us

TrueLink is more than just another social platform. We're a movement to reclaim digital spaces for human connection. A shared vision of ethical technology unites our team of technologists, psychologists, and community builders.

We're headquartered in Pittsburgh but operate as a distributed team across North America and Europe. Our investors include ethical tech funds and impact investors who share our values.

What makes us different isn't just our features, but our foundational philosophy that technology should serve human wellbeing above all else.

Diversity Policy

Our Commitment

TrueLink is committed to building a diverse, inclusive company and community. We believe diverse perspectives create better products and more vibrant communities.

In Our Workplace

We actively recruit and support employees from underrepresented backgrounds, provide unconscious bias training, and maintain equitable compensation practices.

In Our Product

We design for accessibility, conduct regular algorithmic fairness audits, and provide tools for users to find and support diverse voices.

In Our Community

We enforce strict anti-harassment policies, provide content moderation in multiple languages, and actively promote marginalized voices through our recommendation systems.
