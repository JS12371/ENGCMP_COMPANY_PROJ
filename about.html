Brand Story

TrueLink was founded in 2025 by a team of undergrads who became disillusioned with how platforms were designed to exploit human psychology rather than serve genuine needs.

We pledge to creating "true links" between people; connections that are meaningful, intentional, and enriching rather than superficial or manipulative.

Mission Statement

"Our mission is to build meaningful relationships by creating a digital space that reduces loneliness and inspires real-world connections."

Our mission guides every decision we make, from product design to business practices. We measure our success not by engagement metrics or ad revenue, but by the quality of connections our platform facilitates.

About Us

TrueLink is a movement to reclaim digital spaces for their intended use, human connections. This shared vision unites our team of technologists, psychologists, and community builders. We're headquartered in Pittsburgh but operate as a distributed team across North America and Europe. Our investors include ethical tech funds and impact investors who share our values.

1) Beyond the Scroll: Making social media personal again The ever involved role of social media in our society shows no sign of slowing anytime soon. Despite its purpose to bring us together, it has left many more distant and disconnected than ever. Feeds of curated moments and ‘friends’ have shifted the use of social media from its original intention. TrueLink was constructed on the basis that online connections shouldn't be a casualty of the digital age.

2) The necessity of TrueLink To draw people away from chasing unnecessary statistics such as likes and views on other social media platforms, our product forms meaningful connections through purposeful engagement. Too often do current platforms waste user’s time with endless scrolling, while having the interaction aspect of the site as a secondary goal. The desire to flip the script and re-prioritize interactions using a limited amount of the user’s time is a core idea to founding TrueLink.

Diversity Policy

Our Commitment

Diversity inherently aligns with TrueLink’s mission of fostering personal interactions, as these relationships can only be formed when a variety of perspectives are welcomed. Real connections are rooted in diversity, as true human experiences revolve around the multitude of backgrounds, perspectives and identities of our users.

Our Goals

We recognize, welcome, and tailor our experience to individuals in our workspace, community, and in our product across a spectrum, including:

  • Race and Ethnicity
  • Gender Identity and Expression
  • Sexual Orientation
  • Religion and Spiritual Beliefs
  • Socioeconomic Status
  • National Origin
  • Physical and Mental Abilities
  • Life Experiences and Perspectives

Our Actions

To uphold our inclusive beliefs, we enforce strict anti-harassment policies to create the most inclusive interactions possible. Moreover, our team regularly has inclusivity meetings to create a knowledgeable and considerate work environment. Ensuring everyone associated with the app has an important grasp of what diversity means for TrueLink, we are confident in our ability to create an inclusive online environment.

In Our Community

We enforce strict anti-harassment policies, provide content moderation in multiple languages, and actively promote marginalized voices through our recommendation systems.

Our Core Values

Human-Centered Design

We prioritize user wellbeing over engagement metrics, designing features that support rather than exploit human psychology.

Transparency

We're open about our business model, data practices, and algorithmic decisions—no hidden manipulation.

Authentic Connection

We create space for meaningful interactions by limiting superficial engagement features.

Time Well Spent

We help users be intentional with their time through built-in usage reminders and no infinite scroll.

Privacy First

We collect minimal data and never sell user information to advertisers.

Inclusive Community

We actively cultivate diverse, respectful spaces where all voices can be heard.
