Diversity, Equity & Inclusion Policy

Our Commitment

TrueLink is committed to fostering, cultivating, and preserving a culture of diversity, equity, and inclusion. We believe that diverse perspectives lead to better products and more vibrant communities.

In Our Workplace

We actively seek to build a team that reflects the diversity of the communities we serve. Our hiring practices are designed to reduce bias, and we provide ongoing DEI training for all employees.

In Our Product

We design our platform to be accessible to people of all abilities and backgrounds. This includes:

  • Comprehensive accessibility features
  • Content moderation that protects marginalized groups
  • Algorithmic fairness audits to prevent bias
  • Community spaces for underrepresented voices

In Our Community

We enforce strict anti-harassment policies and provide tools for users to shape their own experience. Our moderation team receives specialized training in cultural competency and implicit bias.

Privacy & Data Protection Policy

Data Minimization

We collect only the data necessary to provide our services. Unlike traditional social networks, we don't track your activity across the web or build detailed profiles for advertising.

Transparent Data Use

We clearly explain what data we collect and how it's used. You can access, correct, or delete your data at any time through your account settings.

No Third-Party Sharing

We never sell your data to advertisers or other third parties. Our business model is based on optional premium features, not surveillance capitalism.

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security protocols including encryption, regular audits, and strict access controls to protect your information.

Environmental Policy

Sustainable Operations

We recognize the environmental impact of digital services and are committed to minimizing our footprint through:

  • Carbon-neutral data centers powered by renewable energy
  • Energy-efficient coding practices
  • Remote-first work policies to reduce commuting
  • Partnerships with environmental organizations

Product Design

Our platform is designed to reduce unnecessary data transfer and energy consumption. Features like data compression and dark mode help minimize power usage.

Advocacy

We use our platform to raise awareness about environmental issues and support sustainability initiatives through our community features.

Community Guidelines

Creating a Respectful Space

TrueLink is built on the principle of authentic human connection. To maintain a welcoming environment for all, we ask community members to:

  • Engage with empathy and respect
  • Share authentic content and experiences
  • Respect different perspectives and backgrounds
  • Protect others' privacy and consent

Prohibited Content

We do not allow:

  • Hate speech or discrimination
  • Harassment or bullying
  • Misinformation or manipulated media
  • Spam or manipulative engagement tactics
  • Commercial content outside designated spaces

Enforcement

We combine automated systems with human review to enforce these guidelines. Violations may result in content removal, account restrictions, or permanent bans for severe or repeated offenses.
